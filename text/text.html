About the series

The Myth Adventures series of humorous fantasy novels began in 1978 with "Another Fine Myth". Over the next 24 years Bob wrote eleven more books in the series. You can see the covers in their currently available editions on the Cover Gallery page.

In 2003, Bob started working with Jody Lynn Nye, with whom he had written one previous novel, "License Invoked" (Baen Books, 2001).

Since then, Bob and Jody have written six more Myth Adventures novels and nine short stories.
